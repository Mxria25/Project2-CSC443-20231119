Game Title: Iron Waves

Iron Waves is a first-person survival shooter where the player must survive increasingly difficult waves of enemy robots. Players explore the arena, eliminate enemies, collect points, and purchase upgrades between waves to improve their chances of survival.

Move: WASD
Shoot: Right Mouse Click
Switch Weapons: T
Interact with Shop: Mouse
enemy types: 
Basic Robot: Standard enemy that chases the player
Tank Robot (red hue): Enemy that chases the player (slower but harder to kill than the basic robot)
Turret: Stationary enemy that shoots from a distance

Weapons:
Pistol
Machine Gun
Sniper
Upgrades (Shop):
Heal
Ammo Refill
Damage Increase

Known Bugs:
Minimap behavior near map edges may be inconsistent
